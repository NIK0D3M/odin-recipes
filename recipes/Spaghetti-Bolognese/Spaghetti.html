Spaghetti Recipe

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring spaghetti topped with a rich and savory meat-based tomato sauce, often flavored with garlic, onions, and herbs. Traditionally from Bologna, it is a hearty and comforting meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 400 g (dry, serves 4)
  • Minced Beef: 400 g (can substitute with ground pork or a mix)
  • Onion: 1 medium, finely chopped (about 100 g)
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced (about 5 g)
  • Carrot: 1 medium, finely diced (about 50 g)
  • Celery: 1 stalk, finely diced (about 50 g)
  • Canned Tomatoes: 400 g (or fresh tomatoes, peeled and chopped)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons (for richness)
  • Red Wine: 100 ml (optional, adds depth to the sauce)
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons (for sautéing)
  • Italian Herbs: 1 teaspoon (dried oregano and basil, or fresh herbs if available)
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste
  • Parmesan Cheese: For garnish, freshly grated (optional)
  • Parsley: Fresh, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Step by Step Recipe

    Prepare the Ingredients

  1. Finely chop 1 onion, 2 garlic cloves, 1 carrot, and 1 celery stalk.
  2. Measure 400 g of minced beef and 100 ml of red wine.
  3. Prepare 400 g of canned tomatoes and 2 tablespoons of tomato paste.
  4. Cook 400 g of spaghetti according to package instructions.

    5. Cook the Sauce

  5. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  6. Add the chopped onion, carrot, celery, and garlic. Sauté for 5 minutes until softened.
  7. Add the minced beef to the skillet. Cook for 5-7 minutes until browned.
  8. Stir in the red wine and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  9. Add the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs.
  10. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

    11. Combine the Spaghetti and Sauce

  11. Drain the cooked spaghetti and place it in the skillet with the Bolognese sauce.
  12. Toss to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.

    13. Serve

  13. Plate the spaghetti Bolognese and garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
  14. Serve warm with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad.
